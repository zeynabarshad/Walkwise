Common Pains Insoles Help Relieve

1.Plantar fasciitis

Plantar Fasciitis presents as a pain on the bottom of the heel or mid-foot area and develops gradually over time. May also present pain in the arch of the foot. Can be caused by a biomechanical issue, improper running shoes, use of high heels, increasing activity levels or from tight or weak calf muscles.

2.HEEL PAIN

Heel cups are orthotic devices that support and cushion the heel. Made from soft materials, they alleviate discomfort caused by conditions like plantar fasciitis or heel spurs. By absorbing shock and reducing pressure, heel cups provide pain relief and enhance comfort during activities.

3.Metatarsalgia

Metatarsalgia is a condition causing pain and inflammation in the ball of the foot, often worsened by high-impact activities or improper footwear. Insoles with metatarsal pads provide cushioning and support, redistributing pressure and reducing strain on the metatarsal heads, alleviating discomfort.

4.Arch Pain

Arch pain, or arch strain, is a common issue caused by overuse, flat feet, or high arches. Insoles with arch support provide necessary alignment and cushioning, reducing strain on the plantar fascia and improving foot mechanics. This support alleviates pain and enhances overall foot function.

5. Knee Pain

Knee pain can result from poor foot mechanics, overpronation, or lower body misalignment. Insoles with arch support, heel cups, and shock-absorbing materials correct alignment issues, improve foot mechanics, and reduce knee strain. This support alleviates pain and discomfort, promoting proper alignment and preventing knee pain.

6.Ankle Pain

Insoles redistribute pressure and reduce overload on the ankle joint by providing cushioning and support, alleviating pain and discomfort, and improving mobility during activities like standing, walking, or running.
